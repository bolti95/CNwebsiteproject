My First Tech Blog

Here I am sharing some of the trials and errors of learning to make a website, what I’ve learnt from completing this myself, using HTML, CSS and I’ll lightly touch upon JavaScript and GitHub. At the time of writing, I am completing a course at Code Nation [0.0]. where we’ve worked with JavaScript, HTML and CSS. As part of our final project we had to answer a few questions about IT in business and the process of making websites. I have added the answers to these questions in the pages Unit 1 and Unit 2. All the references I understandto answer these questions are included in my references page.

My name is Izzy Bolt, I graduated from The University of Manchester in 2017 with a bachelor’s degree in Social Anthropology. Since graduating I have worked in environmental business, as a support worker for disabled children and as a freelance artist. It’s fair to say my life experience is varied! My interest in coding comes from a cross-over between my work in TouchDesigner [0.1], using this software to create digital projects, such as VJ projects, and interactive digital art projects. Take a look at some of my graphics work on my Instagram[0.2]. Exploring this software, I was introduced to Python, writing expressions in TouchDesigner to execute commands to effect my visual projects. Within TouchDesigner, I downloaded GLSL examples (written in a language similar to C, take a look at some examples of rendered graphics that were made using GLSL[0.3], gained an interest in shaders [0.4].
